Frequently Asked Questions

What is a “consistent face” from Maison Model X?

▼
A consistent face is a custom AI-generated character whose facial identity stays visually stable across generations, angles, poses, outfits, and lighting inside your favorite diffusion tools. Instead of the face changing every time you regenerate, you get 95%+ face fidelity across images, so the character truly feels like the same person in every shot.

How consistent are the results really?

▼

Maison Model X is built specifically around face identity lock, not just “similar vibes.” Typical packs achieve around 95%+ face consistency across generations when used correctly inside modern diffusion tools.​

In practice, this means:

Facial structure, eye spacing, nose, lips, and jawline stay recognizably the same.

The character remains identifiable even as you change pose, outfit, background, and lighting.​

Extreme stylization or very heavy prompt changes can still nudge identity, but your baseline is far more stable than with ad‑hoc prompting alone.

Can I use this commercially?

▼
Personal use (portfolio, social media) is included in all packages. Commercial use requires a Commercial License (+₹5,000), which includes full commercial rights and resale/client usage allowance. Premium Face packages include commercial licensing by default.

What if I'm not satisfied?

▼
We offer up to 3 revision rounds (Standard) or unlimited revisions (Premium) to ensure 100% satisfaction. If you're still not happy after revisions, we offer full refunds if the work doesn't match your brief or we miss delivery deadlines. No questions asked.

▼
Our reference packs are optimized for all major AI image generators: Midjourney, Google Nano Banana, Seedream 4.0, Stable Diffusion, DALL-E 3, Leonardo AI, and any tool that supports image-to-image or reference uploads. We provide a usage guide with every pack.
