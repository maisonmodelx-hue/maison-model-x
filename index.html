Your Questions, Answered

Do your models look realistic?

▼
Yes. 92% of consumers cannot distinguish our AI-generated fashion imagery from traditional photography in blind testing. Images are hyper-realistic, commercial-ready, with zero obvious "AI look." Your customers will see professional photoshoots, not artifacts.

Is MMX actually better than Diffusion tools?

▼
DIY tools require you to learn prompting, iterate endlessly, manage quality. We do that. You focus on selling. Want to spend 6+ hours weekly mastering AI? DIY works. Want professional images in 48 hours with zero learning curve? We're better.

Is everything customized to my brand, or do I get generic templates?

▼
Everything. Your models, poses, styling, aesthetic, tone—all built from scratch for your brand. We don't recycle. We don't use templates. Your brief becomes our blueprint. We customize your needs, wants, and vision into images that feel native to your brand identity. That's why it takes 24-48 hours. Because it's made for you.

Can I really save 92-97% compared to traditional photographers?

▼
Absolutely. Traditional photography for 50 images costs ₹92,000–₹2,65,000 (photographer + models + location + styling). Maison Model X: ₹7,000–₹17,000. That's 92-95% savings. For a full collection campaign (200 images), save ₹3,30,000–₹9,30,000 annually. Documented in our market research.

Are images unique?

▼
Every image is a unique creation, fully customizable to your specifications with guaranteed commercial usage rights.

Is it ethical to use AI models? Should I disclose this to customers?

▼
Yes, it's ethical. Disclosure is your choice—not legally required. Our data: brands that disclose it see more trust, not less. Frame it as innovation.

What commercial rights do I get with my images?

▼
You own them completely. Use anywhere forever—social, print, ads, packaging. Edit them. Modify them. No attribution needed. It's yours.
