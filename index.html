Your Questions, Answered

Do your models look realistic?

▼
Yes. 92% of consumers cannot distinguish our AI-generated fashion imagery from traditional photography in blind testing. Images are hyper-realistic, commercial-ready, with zero obvious "AI look." Your customers will see professional photoshoots, not artifacts.

Is this actually better than Diffuison tools?

▼
Yes. Three critical differences: **(1) Face Consistency**—Your model's face stays identical across all iterations (Midjourney faces shift constantly); **(2) Commercial Rights**—You own everything outright (DIY tools have unclear licensing); **(3) Professional Service**—Dedicated support, revisions, 24-hour delivery (DIY requires your time and skill). You're paying for a managed service, not a software tool.

Can I really save 92-97% compared to traditional photographers?

▼
Absolutely. Traditional photography for 50 images costs ₹92,000–₹2,65,000 (photographer + models + location + styling). Maison Model X: ₹7,000–₹17,000. That's 92-95% savings. For a full collection campaign (200 images), save ₹3,30,000–₹9,30,000 annually. Documented in our market research.

Are images unique?

▼
Every image is a unique creation, fully customizable to your specifications with guaranteed commercial usage rights.

What usage rights do I get?

▼
Lifetime commercial usage rights. You can use, modify, republish, and commercialize images across all platforms without restrictions, limitations, or ongoing royalties. Use them in your shop, social media, marketing, portfolio—anywhere. No hidden fees. MMX retains zero ownership. Your images are yours in perpetuity.

Can I use the same model across different campaigns?

▼
Yes. Once created, your signature model becomes your brand ambassador. Deploy across multiple seasons, collections, platforms, and campaigns—maintaining consistent brand identity. Same face across all imagery builds customer recognition and trust. This is your competitive advantage.

What if the images don't meet my specifications?

▼
If delivered images meet less than 75% - of your specifications, request a 50% refund within 7 days with evidence. If images aren't delivered within 7 days, receive 100% refund automatically—no questions asked. You're protected both ways.

Can I request revisions after delivery?

▼
Yes, within 7 days. Request reasonable refinements (color adjustments, pose changes, styling tweaks). Redelivered within 24-48 hours at no additional cost. Unlimited revisions included in all tiers (except complete redesigns). No surprises.

How do you ensure face consistency?

▼
When you create your model, we lock in a distinctive face template. Every image you generate maintains that exact same face—ensuring your model is instantly recognizable across all campaigns, seasons, and platforms. This builds brand identity and customer recognition. We manually verify consistency on every project. Unlike DIY tools where faces shift with each generation, your model stays rock-solid.

How do I get started?

▼

1. Choose your tier: Entry-Level (~$72), Professional (~$180), Premium (~$420), Enterprise (~$900), or Bespoke (custom)

2. Fill out intake form: Share brand guidelines, model preferences, styling requirements

3. Make payment: Secure, encrypted transaction (cards, UPI, net banking, PayPal)

4. Receive images: Within 24-48 hours to your email
