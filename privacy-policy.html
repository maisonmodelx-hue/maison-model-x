However, blocking cookies may impair your ability to use certain features of our Service. For instructions on managing cookies, consult your browser's help documentation.

Do Not Track (DNT): We honor Do Not Track browser signals. When a DNT signal is detected, we do not track, plant cookies, or serve personalized advertisements. However, some Service features may not function optimally with DNT enabled.

5. Data Storage, Retention, and Deletion

5.1 Retention Policy

We retain Personal Data and Client Content for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy and to comply with legal obligations.

Specific Retention Periods:

  • Active Accounts: Personal Data is retained as long as your account remains active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days.
  • Order History: Generated Content and project details are retained for a minimum of 24 months to allow re-ordering, comparisons, and access to your portfolio. After 24 months, inactive projects may be archived at your discretion.
  • Payment Records: Transaction data is retained for 7 years in accordance with tax and accounting regulations.
  • Marketing Communications: If you opt out of marketing emails, we retain your email address on our unsubscribe list to prevent future marketing contact (indefinitely, unless you request deletion).
  • Usage Data: Anonymized analytics data is retained indefinitely for trend analysis and service improvement. Identifiable Usage Data is typically purged after 90 days.
  • Support Communications: Email conversations and support tickets are retained for 2 years to maintain context for repeat inquiries and quality assurance.

  • Cease using your data for marketing, analytics, or service improvement purposes
Limitations: We may retain anonymized or aggregated data for analytical purposes, and we may maintain records required by law or for legitimate business necessity (e.g., payment disputes, fraud investigation).

9. Your Data Protection Rights

9.1 Rights Under GDPR (EU/EEA Residents)

If you are a resident of the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following data protection rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the Personal Data we hold about you, including the source of that data and our uses of it.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete Personal Data.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your Personal Data, subject to certain exceptions (e.g., legal retention requirements, ongoing disputes).
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request that we limit the processing of your Personal Data while we address your concerns or verify our legal basis.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your Personal Data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format (e.g., CSV) and have it transmitted directly to another service provider if technically feasible.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your Personal Data for direct marketing or other specified purposes.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for processing at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority regarding our data practices.

9.2 Rights Under CCPA (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: Request what Personal Information we collect, use, share, and sell.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of Personal Information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your Personal Information (we do not sell Personal Data; sharing refers to data transfers for marketing purposes, which you can opt out of).
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate Personal Information.
  • Right to Limit Use: Request that we limit our use of Personal Information to necessary purposes.
