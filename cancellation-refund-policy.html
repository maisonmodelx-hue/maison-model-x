A partial refund is offered because Maison Model X has already expended significant resources including:

While we acknowledge the inconvenience of non-conformance, a full refund is not appropriate when partial work has been completed and resources have been consumed. The 50% refund represents fair compensation for the failure to meet specifications while recognizing the resources already invested.

Important: If the delivered product meets 70% or more of your stated specifications, no refund applies. This is considered acceptable delivery within the parameters of custom AI-generated content, which may involve minor variations inherent to AI technology.

2.2.1 CONFORMANCE ASSESSMENT METHODOLOGY

To determine if your product meets the 70% threshold, conformance will be calculated through objective comparison of delivered output against written specifications using the following criteria:

OBJECTIVE SPECIFICATIONS (Measurable — Counted Toward Conformance)

Objective specifications are concrete, verifiable characteristics that can be unambiguously evaluated:

  • Physical characteristics explicitly specified: ethnicity, age range, body type, height, specific measurements
  • Clothing/styling details: color (specific hex code or clear description), material (cotton, silk, leather), style (casual, formal, sporty), design elements (stripes, patterns, logos), fit (tight, loose, tailored)
  • Technical specifications: image resolution (pixels), file format (JPEG, PNG, TIFF), file size constraints
  • Pose/angle when specifically requested: standing, sitting, three-quarter view, profile
  • Hair attributes when specified: color (specific shade), length (short, medium, long, with measurements), texture (straight, curly, wavy)
  • Background when specified: studio white, outdoor, office setting, specific color or texture

Each objective attribute has equal weighted value in conformance calculation.

SUBJECTIVE SPECIFICATIONS (Interpretive — Excluded from Conformance Calculation)

Subjective specifications are opinion-based interpretations that vary by perspective:

  • Aesthetic quality judgments ("beautiful," "elegant," "sophisticated," "trendy," "professional")
  • Lighting preferences without technical specifications ("dramatic," "soft," "natural")
  • Artistic interpretation ("vibe," "mood," "feeling," "energy")
  • Comparability claims ("better than competitors," "high quality," "stunning," "attractive")
  • Unquantified style terms ("modern," "minimalist," "edgy," "luxurious")

IMPORTANT: If all objective specifications are met, subjective dissatisfaction does NOT trigger refund eligibility. You may request refinements instead.

CONFORMANCE CALCULATION FORMULA

Conformance % = (Objective Specifications Met ÷ Total Objective Specifications Provided) × 100

WORKED EXAMPLE

Your Original Order Specifications:

  1. Ethnicity: Indian (objective)
  2. Age range: 25-30 years (objective)
  3. Body type: athletic/fit (objective)
  4. Hair color: black (objective)
  5. Hair length: shoulder-length (objective)
  6. Outfit color: burgundy (objective)
  7. Pose: standing, three-quarter view (objective)
  8. Background: studio white (objective)
  9. Lighting: "dramatic and professional" (subjective — not counted)
  10. Overall vibe: "sophisticated and modern" (subjective — not counted)

Calculation:

  • Total objective specifications: 8
  • Delivered product meets: Indian ethnicity ✓, 25-30 age ✓, athletic build ✓, black hair ✓, shoulder-length ✓, burgundy outfit ✓, standing pose ✓, background is light gray instead of white ✗ = 7 of 8 met
  • Conformance %: (7 ÷ 8) × 100 = 87.5%
  • Result: EXCEEDS 70% threshold → NO REFUND (acceptable delivery)
  • Available Remedy: You may request refinement of background color at no cost

Alternative Scenario (Lower Conformance):

If the delivered product met only: ethnicity, age, hair color, pose (4 of 8 objective specs) with wrong body type, wrong outfit color, wrong hair length, wrong background:

  • Conformance %: (4 ÷ 8) × 100 = 50%
  • Result: BELOW 70% threshold → ELIGIBLE for 50% partial refund
BURDEN OF PROOF
  • Your Responsibility: When requesting a refund, you must clearly identify:
    1. Your original objective specifications (from order confirmation)
    2. Which specific specifications were NOT met in the delivered product
    3. Evidence supporting your claim (screenshots, description of mismatch)
    4. Your calculated conformance percentage
  • Our Responsibility: We will verify your conformance calculation using the methodology above, review the evidence, and provide a documented decision.
  • Final Authority: Maison Model X retains final determination authority based on objective evidence and this methodology.

2.2.2 AI-GENERATED CONTENT VARIABILITY

You acknowledge that AI-generated outputs may contain inherent minor variations, including but not limited to:

  • Slight differences in facial proportions or feature positioning (e.g., face asymmetries, eye spacing)
  • Minor lighting or shadow variations across different areas of the image
  • Subtle texture or detail differences despite identical specifications
  • Small inconsistencies in geometric precision (e.g., perfectly symmetrical poses may have minor asymmetries)
  • Color saturation or tone variations within acceptable industry standards
  • Micro-expression or micro-gesture variations in facial features
These Minor Variations Are NOT Considered Non-Conformance

Minor variations will NOT trigger refund eligibility if:

  • All objective specifications (ethnicity, body type, styling, pose, etc.) are accurately and substantially represented
  • The product is functionally usable for your intended commercial purpose (e-commerce, portfolio, marketing)
  • The variation does not materially impair the product's utility or professional appearance
  • The variation falls within normal AI output ranges for the specific requested attributes

Examples of Non-Refundable Minor Variations:

  • Skin texture shows slight algorithmic artifacts but face structure matches specifications
  • Hair shows minor strand inconsistencies but color and length are correct
  • Background has slight gradient variations but is the requested color
  • Lighting creates subtle shadow variations but overall brightness matches specifications
  • Pose is precisely as requested but has minor postural variations (1-2 degree angle differences)
Material Variations That Could Trigger Refund Consideration

Material variations (meeting <70% conformance) include:

  • Fundamentally wrong ethnicity despite clear specification (e.g., requested Indian, delivered East Asian)
  • Drastically different body type from requested (e.g., requested athletic, delivered plus-size)
  • Outfit/styling completely wrong or mismatched (e.g., requested formal black suit, delivered casual t-shirt)
  • Wrong pose entirely (e.g., requested standing, delivered sitting)
  • Background completely different (e.g., requested studio white, delivered outdoor forest)
  • Hair color entirely wrong (e.g., requested black, delivered blonde)
  • Technical failure (file corruption, non-delivery, unreadable format)
  • Multiple objective specifications missed (resulting in <70% conformance)
How This Protects Both Parties
  • For Customers: Ensures you have clear expectations about AI limitations while preventing frivolous refund claims
  • For MMX: Provides defensible, objective criteria against chargebacks and consumer complaints

Due to our rapid delivery model (24-48 hours), most orders enter production within 12 hours of placement. The time-based cancellation windows balance customer rights with operational realities of AI model generation—resource allocation occurs immediately upon order confirmation.

These windows provide reasonable opportunity for customer reflection while ensuring business operational feasibility.

Cancellation Request Within 12 Hours (Before Production Start):

All refunds are processed as source refunds, meaning the refund is credited back to the original payment method used to make the purchase. This policy is mandated by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) and payment gateway providers (Razorpay, etc.) to prevent fraud and money laundering.

Examples:

  • If you paid with a Visa credit card, the refund will be credited to that same Visa credit card
  • If you paid via UPI using Google Pay, the refund will be credited to the same Google Pay UPI account
  • If you paid via net banking from HDFC Bank, the refund will be credited to the same HDFC Bank account

6.2 No Alternative Refund Methods

We do not provide refunds via:

  • Store credit or vouchers
  • Cash payments
  • Bank transfers to a different account

In cases where a 50% partial refund is approved:

9. REFINEMENTS VS. REFUNDS

It is important to distinguish between refinements and refunds:

9.1 Refinements

  • Refinements are modifications or adjustments to the delivered product to better align with your specifications
  • Refinements are provided at no additional cost within the 7-day reporting period
  • Refinement requests are the preferred remedy for minor non-conformance issues (product meets 70% or more of specifications)
  • Reasonable refinement requests include:
    • Minor color adjustments (skin tone, hair color, outfit color)
    • Small style modifications within original specifications
    • Pose or angle adjustments
    • Accessories or prop changes
    • Attribute clarifications (size, style consistency)

For full details on refinements, see Section 4: Products, Delivery, and Refinements in our Terms of Service.

9.2 Refunds

  • Refunds are monetary compensation for non-delivery, major non-conformance, or other qualifying issues
  • Refunds are issued only under the specific circumstances outlined in Section 2 of this Policy
  • Refunds are the remedy for major failures (product meets less than 70% of specifications) or non-delivery

9.3 Choosing Between Refinements and Refunds

  • If your delivered product meets 70% or more of your objective specifications, you should request refinements (not refunds)
  • If your delivered product meets less than 70% of your objective specifications, you may request a 50% partial refund
  • If your product is not delivered within 7 days, you may request a full refund

9.3.1 You Cannot Request Both Refinements AND Refunds

You must choose one remedy per issue:

  • If Issue is Minor (≥70% Conformance): Request refinements, not refunds
  • If Issue is Major (<70% Conformance): You may request 50% refund instead of refinements, but not both
  • Once Refinement is Accepted: You waive your right to refund for that same issue

This ensures fair process and prevents sequential exploitation of both remedies for identical problems.

9.4 When You Should Request Refinements Instead of Refunds

Choose refinements if:

  • The delivered model is close to what you want (meets 70%+ of objective specs)
  • You have minor adjustment requests (color, pose, styling)
  • You believe the issue is a minor interpretation difference
  • You want to improve the result without losing your investment

Choose refunds only if:

  • The delivered model is dramatically different from specifications
  • Major specifications were completely missed
  • The product is genuinely unusable for your intended purpose
