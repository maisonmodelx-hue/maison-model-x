Frequently Asked Questions

What is an AI model reference pack?

▼
An AI model reference pack is a collection of 8–10 high-resolution images showing the same photorealistic human face from multiple angles (front, side, ¾, full body, close-up) and expressions. You use these as reference images in AI tools like Midjourney's Character Reference feature, Nano Banana prompts, or Stable Diffusion LoRA training — ensuring your AI-generated outputs maintain the same face identity every time.

How long does delivery take?

▼
Standard delivery: 18–24 hours after your brief is submitted. We also offer Quick Face (24–48 hours), Premium Face (12–18 hours), and Rush Delivery add-on (12 hours for +₹2,000). You'll receive progress updates via email within 12 hours of order placement.

Can I use this commercially?

▼
Personal use (portfolio, social media) is included in all packages. Commercial use requires a Commercial License (+₹5,000), which includes full commercial rights and resale/client usage allowance. Premium Face packages include commercial licensing by default.

What if I'm not satisfied?

▼
We offer up to 3 revision rounds (Standard) or unlimited revisions (Premium) to ensure 100% satisfaction. If you're still not happy after revisions, we offer full refunds if the work doesn't match your brief or we miss delivery deadlines. No questions asked.

What AI tools are supported?

▼
Our reference packs are optimized for all major AI image generators: Midjourney, Google Nano Banana, Seedream 4.0, Stable Diffusion, DALL-E 3, Leonardo AI, and any tool that supports image-to-image or reference uploads. We provide a usage guide with every pack.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

▼
Yes! Bulk discounts available: 3 models (10% off), 5 models (15% off), 10+ models (20% off). Contact us for custom agency packages or white-label partnerships.

What's your refund policy?

▼
Full refunds available if we miss delivery deadlines or if work doesn't match your brief after all revision rounds. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days. We're confident in our quality, so refunds are rare.
